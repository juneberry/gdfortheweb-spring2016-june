Vinca Kruk ✜ joined forces with Daniel van der Velden ✜ in 2007, founding Metahaven, a studio combining design, research and writing, and they have been working and publishing together since. Vinca has been a Tutor of Editorial Design and Design Critique at ArtEZ Academy of Arts in Arhem since 2007 and was Senior Lecturer at Otis College for Art and Design, Los Angeles (2012) and mentor at the IM Master program at the Design Academy (2010).
